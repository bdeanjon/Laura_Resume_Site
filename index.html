About Me

I was born and raised in Ottawa, Ontario. In 2007, I graduated from Concordia Univerisity. I spent 7 years in Korea, teaching as well as mentoring teacchers and children as my duties increased.

In 2018, I moved to Montreal in order to pursue my passion in higher education. I was hired as an ESL teacher/mentor to foreign students who had recently moved to Canada. I taught them English and aided them with adjusting to western life in their new home country. Many had left very difficult situations and needed an instructor to not only teach them English, but to also help facilitate their transition into Western Universities and employment.
